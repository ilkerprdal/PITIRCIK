## 2. Application Description

Pıtırcık is a mobile application designed to track the health, development, and daily activities of babies and children. The application offers the following features:

- Family member management
- Health tracking (vaccinations, medications, check-ups, fever)
- Growth tracking
- Feeding, sleep, diaper change records
- Photo albums
- Notes
- Monthly reports
- Reminder notifications
- Google Drive backup (optional)
