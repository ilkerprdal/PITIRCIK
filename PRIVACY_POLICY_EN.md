# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 2024

## 1. Introduction

Pıtırcık application ("We", "Our", or "App") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how our application collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data.

## 2. Data Collected

### 2.1. Personal Information
The application collects and stores the following personal information:

- **Family Member Information:**
  - Name
  - Date of birth
  - Gender
  - Photos

- **Health Information:**
  - Vaccination records
  - Medication usage information
  - Medical check-up records
  - Fever tracking
  - Growth data (height, weight)
  - Dental health records
  - Symptom and finding records

- **Daily Activity Information:**
  - Feeding records
  - Sleep records
  - Diaper change records
  - Notes
  - Photo albums

### 2.2. Technical Information
- Device information (model, operating system version)
- Application usage data
- Error reports (for application performance)

### 2.3. Permissions
The application requests the following permissions:

- **Camera:** To take photos of babies/children
- **Gallery Access:** To save and view photos
- **Notifications:** For reminders (medication, check-ups, vaccinations)
- **Internet Access:** For Google Drive backup (optional)

## 3. Use of Data

Collected data is used for the following purposes:

- To track family members' health and development
- To send reminder notifications
- To generate monthly reports
- To improve user experience
- To fix application errors

## 4. Data Storage

### 4.1. Local Storage
All data is stored locally on your device (AsyncStorage). Data exists only on your device and is not sent to our servers.

### 4.2. Google Drive Backup (Optional)
Users can optionally backup their data to their Google Drive accounts. In this case:

- Data is stored in the user's own Google Drive account
- Data is stored in encrypted format
- Only the user can access their own data
- Google Drive backup is completely optional

### 4.3. Data Deletion
Users can delete their data from within the application at any time. When the application is uninstalled, all local data is removed from the device.

## 5. Data Sharing

**Important:** We do not share user data with third parties. Data:

- Is not sold to advertising companies
- Is not sent to analytics companies
- Is not transferred to third-party services

**Exceptions:**
- If the user uses the Google Drive backup feature, data is stored in the user's own Google Drive account (Google's privacy policy applies)
- When required by legal obligations (very rare cases)

## 6. Data Security

- All data is stored in encrypted format on your device
- Google Drive backups are made in encrypted format
- Internet connections are protected with HTTPS protocol
- The application is regularly updated and security vulnerabilities are closed

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our application is designed for tracking children's health and development. However:

- The application is not directly used by children under 13
- The application is used by parents/guardians
- Children's personal information is stored with the consent and control of parents
- It is designed in compliance with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements

## 8. User Rights

Users have the following rights:

- **Right to Access Data:** You can access your own data
- **Right to Rectify Data:** You can correct incorrect information
- **Right to Delete Data:** You can delete your data
- **Right to Data Portability:** You can export your data (in JSON format)
- **Right to Object:** You can object to certain data processing activities

## 9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our application does not use cookies or third-party tracking technologies.

## 10. Third-Party Services

The application uses the following third-party services:

- **Google OAuth:** For user authentication (optional)
- **Google Drive API:** For data backup (optional)
- **Expo Notifications:** For sending notifications

These services have their own privacy policies that apply.

## 11. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be notified within the application. The current version of the policy can always be found on this page.
